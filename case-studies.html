Mark – Building Thought Leadership in Leadership

Challenge

Mark, a leadership coach targeting early-stage leaders, wanted to establish authority on LinkedIn. His content wasn't reaching the right audience, and his engagement was stuck at 1.5K impressions per post.

Solution

We crafted a 3-post-per-week strategy focused on actionable leadership insights, paired with an engagement plan to boost reach. Each post combined personal stories with practical takeaways.

Results (3 Months)

10K+ Average Impressions
9% Engagement Rate
3x Follower Growth

Mark is now recognized in his niche and regularly receives speaking and consulting invites — many sparked by his content.
