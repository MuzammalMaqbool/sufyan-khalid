About Me

Hey, I'm Sufyan — a copywriter & ghostwriter for high-trust brands.

I started out in the trenches of hardcore direct response — studying the greats like Gary Halbert, Stefan Georgi, and Dan Lok. That's where I learned how to write copy that grabs attention, builds emotional momentum, and gets people to act.

But over time, I saw a bigger opportunity: helping founders, executives, and creators build brands that people trust — not just click on. That's when I went all-in on LinkedIn ghostwriting — and never looked back.

Today, I help clients clarify their voice, grow their audience, and turn silent lurkers into paying customers through a mix of:

  • Strategy
  • Storytelling
  • Smart-as-hell structure
